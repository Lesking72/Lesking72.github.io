Ticket/Seating Information

Priority Seating (Black Ticket)

  • All individuals sitting in priority seating MUST wear a wristband and check in together at the priority gate.
  • All priority tickets have an assigned seating location. You can bring chairs or blankets, umbrellas and chairs with a canopy are not permitted.
  • The 4 priority ticket holders must sit together in the priority section corresponding to graduates location in the stands.
  • Wristbands aren't getting distributed to graduates until the night before, for some reason.

Extra Seating (White Ticket)

  • All individuals with white guest tickets will be sitting behind the priority guests. This seating is not assigned and will be first come seating.
  • You will need to bring a chair or blanket if you choose to sit on the field. Umbrellas and canopy chairs are prohibited.
  • Seating will be in groups of 2 in the stands or groups of 4 on the field. Bleacher seating is limited
  • Distancing between groups will be expected.

All seating

  • Anyone not sitting in the appropriate area, refusing to maintain distance from other groups, or blocking others' view will be escorted out.
  • ALL gates will close at 8:50 AM. Entrance to the seating areas will take some time so plan accordingly.

Entering graduation

  • EVERYONE in line must stay 6 feet apart from those not in their group and MUST wear a mask
  • EVERYONE MUST have their temperature taken before entering the gate and complete the CDC questionnaire
  • NO food or drinks will be allowed in the gate
  • No balloons, umbrellas or objects that can block the view of others are allowed.
  • If you bring a bag or purse it MUST be searched before you can enter
  • Please do not bring unnecessary items so we can move our entrance lines quickly and safely
